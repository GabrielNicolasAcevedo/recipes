Pizza

Description

I will guide you step-by-step on how to make your own pizza at home and invite your friends over.

They will love it and so will you!

Ingredients

  • 1kg flour
  • 5g wheat
  • 5g sugar
  • 20g salt
  • 40g oil
  • 700cc water (35Â°)

Steps

  1. First we will turn the oven to max
  2. Add the ingredients into mixing bowl
  3. Mix until you get a clean dough
  4. Separate into 4-5 ball looking dough and let leaven in warm place for 10min.
  5. Place on pizza pans and stretch the dough into a pizza shape. Leaven for 10min.
  6. Add tomato sauce and straight to the oven for 8min
  7. Add cheese and desired ingredients and into the oven for 3-4minutes
  8. Enjoy your pizza!
